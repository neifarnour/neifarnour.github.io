About Me

Hi everyone! I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Digital Research Center of Sfax (CRNS) in Tunisia and a member of SM@RTS Laboratory , working with the SmartVision team, which focuses primarily on computer vision and deep learning projects. I earned my PhD in Computer Science in 2024 from the National School of Engineers of Sfax (ENIS), where I was a member of the ReDCAD Laboratory.

My research interests include artificial intelligence, machine learning, and deep learning, with a particular focus on generative models and their applications. I am also interested in exploring innovative approaches for processing and analyzing complex data, including biomedical signals, images, and other forms of multimodal information. Beyond technical development, I aim to contribute to AI methods that are efficient, interpretable, and impactful across various real-world domains.
